Why it feels like nothing

Engineered at every contact point.

Most glasses fail where they touch you. Dozi re-engineers all four contact zones to stay under the discomfort threshold — even after hours.

8–12cm²

Saddle bridge

A single continuous silicone saddle spreads weight over 4–6× the area of nose pads, so there's no pinch point on the bridge.

≤0.50N

Hinge-free temples

No hinges to clamp or snap. The silicone's own spring tension holds the frame gently — well below the 0.90N discomfort threshold.

<20g/cm²

Silk brow band

The largest contact zone is lined in mulberry silk and rests barely-there on the brow — cool, breathable, moisture-wicking.

<25g

Featherweight

Total assembly under 25 grams — lighter than most reading glasses, so there's almost nothing to feel against your face.
