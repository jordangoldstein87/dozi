Good to know

Questions, answered.

Can I really fall asleep in them?

Yes — that's the whole point. The hinge-free silicone frame flexes instead of pressing, and the silk lining stays cool against your skin. There are no hard joints to dig in when you're lying on your side.

How do prescriptions work?

Choose the Rx lens at checkout and you'll be guided through uploading your prescription. We make lenses in polycarbonate, Trivex, and High-Index, with optional blue-light, photochromic, and anti-reflective coatings.

What's the difference between Silicone and Silk-Lined?

Both have the same soft-touch silicone frame and seamless wraparound lens. Silk-Lined adds a silk interior where the frame meets your skin — cooler, more breathable, and our most-loved pick for all-night wear.

What sizes are available?

Every Dozi comes in Small and Large. If you're between sizes, our 30-night trial means you can swap risk-free.

Do you offer a trial?

Every order includes a 30-night risk-free trial. Rest in them, sleep in them, and if they're not for you, send them back.
